A Legacy Lasting Aeons

We here at Fairy Fields have a rich history dating all the way back to 1200BCE. Our story began when Glorbascius, the founding father of our beloved home, decided to claim a local grove in Maine as his reverly ground. Through a mixture of ambrosia, sweat, and appeasing the local dryads, he managed to grow the tiny grove into the wonderful and very exclusive vacationing property enjoyed by dozens of very priviledged individuals!

Over the years we expanded our services from a simple grove puddle for frolicking to an entire winter resort, featuring dozens of luxurious amenities. Ranging from relaxing hot springs with the local nature spirits present to provide massages, to hunting local fauna with centaur guides, everyone has something they can participate in and make memories that will last a lifetime.
